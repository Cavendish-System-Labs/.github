# Cavendish System Labs

**A lab. A hangout. A place to build things.**

---

## Who we are

Cavendish System Labs is a free, open, no-pressure collaborative space for people who like to code. No formal structure. No mandatory stack. No gatekeeping.

You can be a student, a hobbyist, a professional, or someone who just started programming last week — doesn't matter. If you like building things and want a place to do it with others, you're in the right place.

Named after **Henry Cavendish** — the obsessive, reclusive scientist who measured the mass of the Earth from his backyard. We inherited the curiosity, not the reclusiveness.

## Ground rules

- **No mandatory language or framework.** Python, Rust, JavaScript, Go, Bash, brainfuck — your call.
- **No mandatory skill level.** Beginners ship here too.
- **No mandatory schedule.** Contribute when you feel like it.
- **Be respectful.** That's the only real rule.

---

## Want to join or collab?

Open an issue, fork a repo, or just reach out. No application, no interview, no waiting list.

Show up and build something.
